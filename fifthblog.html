Market Sizing and ADOPTABLE's Potential

Understanding market sizing is crucial for any startup. In this article, we explore the importance of evaluating the Total Addressable Market (TAM), Serviceable Available Market (SAM), and Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM) and how these concepts help us at ADOPTABLE assess our potential growth opportunities.

Market Sizing: TAM, SAM, and SOM

Market sizing allows us to evaluate the potential of our product in a specific market segment. For ADOPTABLE, determining the market size helps us understand how much of the pet adoption industry we can reach and capture. Below is a breakdown of these terms:

  • Total Addressable Market (TAM): This refers to the overall revenue opportunity available if we could capture 100% of the market. For pet adoption, this would include the total number of pets needing homes in the Philippines and beyond, as well as the total consumer base of potential adopters.
  • Serviceable Available Market (SAM): This is the portion of TAM that we can target based on our service capabilities, geographical reach, and resources. It focuses on markets that we can realistically serve, such as users in Cagayan de Oro or other Mindanao regions where the locality of pet adoption apps is scarce.
  • Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM): This is the realistic market share that we can capture in the short to medium term. Itâ€™s determined by factors like brand recognition, marketing efforts, and competition. For ADOPTABLE, this would be the portion of the market that we can effectively acquire given our current resources and strategies.

Relevance to ADOPTABLE

For us at ADOPTABLE, market sizing is not just about understanding the size of the pet adoption industry, but also identifying where we stand in comparison to our competitors and what strategies we need to adopt to capture our share of the market.

By calculating TAM, SAM, and SOM, we can ensure that we are targeting the right market segments and using our resources effectively. We can also identify growth opportunities, track our progress, and refine our business strategies as we expand.

Key Takeaways for ADOPTABLE

  • We should calculate our TAM to understand the total opportunity in the pet adoption market.
  • The SAM will help us focus on markets we can realistically serve with our current capabilities.
  • By determining the SOM, we can establish a clear growth target and plan our marketing and operational efforts accordingly.
  • Understanding market sizing ensures that we allocate our resources efficiently and have a clear roadmap for our expansion in the pet adoption sector.

Understanding and applying market sizing concepts such as TAM, SAM, and SOM will help us carve out our place in the competitive landscape of pet adoption platforms.
