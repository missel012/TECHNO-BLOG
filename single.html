Understanding Pet Adoption Through Natasha's Perspective

In our journey to create a platform for pet adoption, I had the opportunity to hear from Natasha Sofia Bonachita, a resident of Gusa, Cagayan de Oro City, who loves cats but currently doesn't own one. Her insights shed light on the practical considerations and emotional connections that influence pet adoption.

Customer Job: Natasha mentioned that the most important factor for her when adopting a pet would be size. She would want to know how big the pet might grow, whether it's a kitten or fully grown, to ensure that the pet could comfortably live in her home. Additionally, she emphasized the need for information on the pet's health and any special needs they might have.

Before adopting, Natasha believes in thorough preparation. She mentioned that she would gather all the essentials, like food, cat litter, and a bed, to make sure the pet could settle in comfortably as soon as they arrived.

Customer Pain: When it comes to challenges in the adoption process, Natasha expressed concerns about the complexity of filling out forms and the potential for hidden fees. She said she would prefer a straightforward and transparent process, free of any surprises.

One of her main concerns about adopting a pet is the pet’s health and whether the animal would adapt well to its new environment.

Customer Gain: When asked about what she would like to see in a pet adoption app, Natasha suggested detailed pet profiles that showcase not only the animal's health but also their likes, dislikes, and temperament. She also mentioned how useful it would be to have filters to help find the right pet, along with an option for real-time chat with shelters.

Insights: Natasha’s insights reflect a practical yet compassionate approach to pet adoption, showing how important it is to have all the right information and resources available when making such a significant decision. Her love for animals shines through in her thoughtful consideration of what it takes to welcome a new pet into her home.
